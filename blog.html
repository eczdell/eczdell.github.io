The Landscape Of Life

Posted 2 days ago
share
Share via social media

Life is More or less Like the paintings Of Vincent - Mysterious, Raw And profound. The more we understand The more complex it will be. Foraging the meaning of life is Just like a snake chasing its tail In a circle with radius, half of its size. We would be caged, Inside the inescapable bars Of our foundational knowledge By the time We understand the meaning. Life is an unplannable voyage With no destination; All routes are true; What matters is The angle between The hands of your heart's compass and The direction you are walking on. Life is unorganizable. We should learn the art of surfing On its arduous waves; Acknowledging Its primordial attribute. Composed by Mr. ECZ August 29,2020
